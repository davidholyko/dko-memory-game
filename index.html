Instructions

Click two cards to match! The objective of the game is to match all the cards. There are four unique cards to match. Once a card is matched with the same card, those cards can no longer be matched with any other card and remain static. If a card does not match another card, both cards are flipped over on the next click. If you try to match a card with itself, it will output text saying you cannot match a card with itself!
